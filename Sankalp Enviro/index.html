About Us

Sankalp Enviro is a student organization under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University. The name Sankalp Enviro drives its name from words, in which Sankalp means "to determine" and Enviro refers "environment”. As its name suggests, Sankalp Enviro's objective is to spread awareness on social and environmental related issues.”.

The team of Sankalp has been thriving to provide practical solutions to these issues. It intends to bring along the masses together to protect the environment and carry out work at the ground level. We as a team of Sankalp like to be the change that we want to see. We started our journey on September 21st 2014 with few students of Lovely Professional University. We have been striving hard to bring change in society around us regarding the social responsibility and environment. Now we have grown to a good strength of 80+ from diverged culture but united for a common cause. As our helping hands grown the deeds we did also have grown big.
